    Company Description

    Founded in July 2011, Open Lectures represents an idea - the belief that education should and will be free. Since then, Open Lectures has been helping students of all backgrounds in Singapore acquire top quality resources in education by providing online lectures delivered by top Singapore Junior College graduates. With 60 students and over 200 lectures recorded, Open Lectures eventually aims to deliver a lecture on the moon.

  • Executives

    Linan Qiu

    Founder and Chief Executive Officer

    Linan's job is to make irresistable offers and y'know, run the company. A student at Columbia University, he has somehow managed to make the entire team work for free. He cites his Communist roots. Linan leads the design of the openlectures service and development of its core infrastructure.

    Zhang Yitao

    Founder and Chief Financial Officer

    Yitao sells ice to the eskimos and charges them a delivery fee. He makes his home somewhere in-between Stanford University and Silicon Valley, where he acquires and applies his vast experience in entrepreneurship. He leads the finance, internationalization and facilities aspects of life as our staff know it.

    Lim Mingjie, Kenneth

    Chief Information Officer

    Being CIO means Kenneth manages the running code riot that powers the openlectures site. He is responsible for the myriad of "ghetto" techniques that deliver lectures from behind the scenes. All crazy concepts are copyright him.

    Law Kang Jie

    Chief Communications Officer

    Kang Jie talks to everyone. When he's done with them, they normally fall irrevocably in love with what we do here. A man with a preference for all things creative, ask him about fashion when you meet him.

    Tek Yong Jian

    Chief Operating Officer

    Yong Jian started out as the one-man video editing team. While his responsibilities have expanded to include the day-to-day aspects of the company, he still makes it a point to go over every minute of footage just to ensure that sound and video look professional. He does not own much of a computer.

    Lee Cheng Feng Gary

    Chief Academic Officer

    Gary commands our veritable formation of lecturers, and provides the overall direction for development of content. He oversees curriculum planning, manpower matters and "anything else". He is currently single and available.
