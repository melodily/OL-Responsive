Open Lectures was built and designed to be user-friendly and open. Since we're here to offer something for free, we'd like to do it with as little strings attached as possible. If you'd still like to read the full hullabaloo, you'll find them all here.

Teachers

You're free to screen our lectures in class, refer to them, include them in your lesson plans, or assign them as material for your students. Do show our lectures in their entirety.

Students

Do almost anything you will. Watch it, refer to it, argue over it, talk to us about it. All we ask is that you share it with your friends if you've found it useful.

Organizations

Screen our lectures at public venues, come up with supporting material for them, recommend them to your staff. If you'd like to do more, let us know.

Need to cite us? Use Open Lectures - Free, quality education for everyone (http://openlectures.sg)
